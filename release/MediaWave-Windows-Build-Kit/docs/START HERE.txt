============================================================
  WELCOME TO MEDIAWAVE 2000
============================================================

MediaWave turns your video library into a simulated TV
experience — channels, commercial breaks, and all.

QUICK START
-----------
1. Open the MediaWave folder that was created in your
   Documents folder (or press "Open MediaWave Folder"
   inside the app).

2. Inside the Channels subfolder, create one folder per
   channel. Each channel folder holds your video files.

   Example:
     Channels/
       HBO Classic/
         Shows/
         Movies/
       Comedy Central/
         Specials/

3. Launch MediaWave, press "Choose Catalog…", and select
   your Channels folder.

4. Press "Watch TV ▶" to start.

YOUR MEDIA FOLDER
-----------------
MediaWave creates this folder the first time you launch:

  Documents/MediaWave/
    Channels/         ← put your channel folders here
    Commercials/      ← optional commercial video clips
    Music/            ← optional RadioWaveTV music
    Fonts/            ← optional custom fonts
    Themes/           ← optional custom themes
    Converted/        ← MediaWave Converter output lands here
    Settings/         ← app settings (don't delete)
    Cache/            ← thumbnails and metadata (safe to clear)

You are NOT required to use this folder. You can choose any
folder on any drive as your catalog — an external drive, a
NAS share, whatever you like. MediaWave remembers your
choice.

INCLUDED APPS
-------------
  MediaWave.exe          - the TV experience
  MediaWave Converter.exe - convert videos to MediaWave format
