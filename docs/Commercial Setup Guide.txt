============================================================
  COMMERCIAL SETUP GUIDE
============================================================

MediaWave can play real commercial breaks between episodes,
just like broadcast TV.

WHAT YOU NEED
-------------
A folder of short video clips (commercials, bumpers, promos,
station IDs). These can be real ad recordings, custom clips,
or anything you want.

Recommended: convert them with MediaWave Converter first
for the smoothest playback.

SETTING UP YOUR COMMERCIALS FOLDER
------------------------------------
1. Put your commercial video files in a folder.
   You can organize them however you like — flat or nested.

   Example:
     Commercials/
       1980s/
         ad_01.mp4
         ad_02.mp4
       Bumpers/
         bumper_01.mp4

2. In MediaWave, open "Advanced Config" from the main screen.

3. Find the "Commercials" section.

4. Enable commercials and select your Commercials folder.

5. Choose how often you want commercial breaks to appear.

6. Save and reload your catalog.

DEFAULT LOCATION
----------------
If you are using the MediaWave Documents folder, a
Commercials/ subfolder is created there automatically.
You can use it as-is or choose any other folder.

COMMERCIAL TIMING MODES
------------------------
  Between Episodes     - break plays between each episode
  Timed Mid-Roll       - break plays every N minutes

DENSITY SETTINGS
----------------
  Light     - 1-2 ads per break
  Normal    - 2-4 ads per break
  Heavy     - 4-6 ads per break

PER-CHANNEL OVERRIDES
---------------------
In Advanced Config > My Catalog, you can override
commercial settings for individual channels.

DISABLING COMMERCIALS
---------------------
Uncheck "Enable Commercials" in Advanced Config at any time.
The commercial library is not deleted — just paused.
